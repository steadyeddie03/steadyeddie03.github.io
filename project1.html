The 116 Clique

Overview

The 116 Clique is a hip hop group based in Atlanta, Georgia. All of the artists are signed to the same record label, Reach Records. They were formed in 2006 by Lecrae, Tedashii and Trip Lee. Andy Mineo's introduction to Reach Records came in the form of Lecrae's song, Background. After that song, Andy was signed to Reach. Gawvi produced most of the tracks for Lecrae and the other artists of Reach Records. He was signed to Reach Records as an artist in 2016. 1K Phew was featured in Lecrae's song, Hammer Time, and impressed Lecrae and got signed shortly thereafter. 1K Phew is known for always keeping it 1000 on all of his tracks.

Mission Statement

Their mission is to spread the word of God through their music to reach as many people as they can, one song at a time. They are rebels, misfits. Quite simply, they are different and don't fit in the box that encompasses society. They take their name from Romans 1:16 which says:

“I am not ashamed of the Gospel, because it is the power of God for salvation for everyone who believes”.
